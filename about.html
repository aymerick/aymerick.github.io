I'm Aymerick, a software developer who lives near Rennes, France.

I work remotely as a full stack web developer since June 2008 at Fotonauts, a company founded by a group of ex-Apple engineers to create Fotopedia, a photo community website.

I met Fotonauts founders while working remotely as a contractor for Apple. During that mission I implemented the SyncML protocol in the iSync synchronisation tool shipped with MacOS X. That protocol permitted to add support for tens of new mobile devices for Mac users.

Before that, I worked four years in the telecom industry, mainly on protocols on the server side (WAP , MMS, EMS...). I developed too on embeded devices for clients like Mitsubishi and Philips.
