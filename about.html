I'm Aymerick, a software developer who lives near Rennes, France.

I work remotely as a full stack web developer at Fotonauts since June 2008. Fotonauts was founded by a group of ex-Apple engineers.

I met Fotonauts founders while working remotely as a contractor for Apple for a two-years mission during which I implemented the SyncML protocol in the iSync tool shipped with MacOS X. That protocol permitted to add synchronisation support for tens of new mobile devices for Mac users.

Before that, I worked four years in the telecom industry, mainly on protocols on the server side (WAP , MMS, EMS...). I developed too on embeded devices for clients like Mitsubishi and Philips.
